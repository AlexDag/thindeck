Architecture

This document specifies architecture and design decisions made in relation to the Thindeck.com project.

Section 2 lists basic assumptions made after investigation of the provided business case description. Section 3 includes two class diagrams showing the packaging and class design. Section 4 explains separation of software components, and the interaction between them and third-parties. Section 5 describes a proposed physical layout of the major tiers of the system under development (SuD). Section 6 gives internal details of major use case implementations. Section 7 lists the three most severe technical risks identified, analyzed, and planned. Section 8 lists all important technical decisions made during architecture and design.
